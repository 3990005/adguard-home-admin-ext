Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

AdGuard Home Admin ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

The App operates entirely on your device and communicates directly with your self-hosted AdGuard Home server(s). We do not have access to, collect, store, or transmit any of your data to external servers.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Server Configurations: The addresses and names of your AdGuard Home servers are stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • Authentication Credentials: Your server login credentials are stored securely in the iOS Keychain, Apple's encrypted credential storage system.

This data never leaves your device except to communicate directly with your own AdGuard Home server(s).

Network Communications

The App communicates only with the AdGuard Home server(s) that you configure. These connections are:

  • Made directly from your device to your server
  • Not routed through any third-party services
  • Not monitored, logged, or analyzed by us

We recommend using HTTPS connections to your AdGuard Home server for enhanced security.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, behavior, or any other activity within the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs that collect user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. Your information stays entirely on your device and your own server(s).

Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • Credentials are stored in the iOS Keychain with hardware-level encryption
  • No data is transmitted to our servers (we don't have any)
  • The App supports secure HTTPS connections to your AdGuard Home server

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. The App is a technical tool for managing self-hosted DNS servers and is intended for users who operate their own AdGuard Home installations.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You can view your stored servers in the App at any time
  • You can delete any server configuration from within the App
  • Uninstalling the App removes all stored data from your device

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
